PRE-MATURE BALDNESS

OVERVIEW

If you are in the early stage of your life, such as your teens or early twenties, and are losing a huge amount of hair every day, it could be premature baldness. Hair thinning is the first stage of premature baldness. When you lose hair strands fast, your scalp becomes more visible in between your hair shafts. Reports show that the majority of people suffering from extreme hair loss are in their mid-twenties.If premature baldness is diagnosed at an early stage, the hair loss can be reversible.



SIGNS & SYMPTOMS



  • The hair on your temples gradually starts thinning.
  • Hair thinning takes place on the frontal scalp.
  • Bald patches in your scalp appear.
  • A huge amount of hair loss happens at once, while you are combing
        or washing your hair.
  • Scaly patches appear all over your scalp.




CAUSES



  • Dihydrotestosterone(DHT)
  • Alopecia Areata
  • Lupus
  • Thyroid Disorders

TREATMENTS



  • Medical Treatment
  • Hair Transplant Surgery
  • Home Remedies


PRECAUTIONS



  • Do not ignore the signs of premature baldness. If you notice any symptoms, consult a doctor. Early-stage medication will help you cure the condition more effectively.
  • Try to avoid hair styling and heating tools as much as you can.
  • Use natural products and avoid chemical-based hair care products. Choose hair care products that suit your hair.
  • Keep your overall health in check. Underlying diseases such as thyroid, or many autoimmune diseases are major causes of hair loss.
  • If you are taking certain medication and noticing sudden hair fall,check with your doctor. It can be a side effect of the medicine. Ask your doctor to provide alternate medication.
